FAQ

Q1. When can we book the service in advance?

Can be booked from 3 months before the day.

Q2. What services are include?

Meals, cleaning the room.

Short stay: health check, one dog walk.

Staying over: health check, two dog walks (morning & night).

Q3. Can we leave pet carriers with you?

Yes, you can bring your carrier and leave it to be stored in our secure room.

Q4. Do you have insurance?

Yes, we do.

It covers mainly for the case of your dog being injured accidentally or an incident like your dog biting other dogs.
